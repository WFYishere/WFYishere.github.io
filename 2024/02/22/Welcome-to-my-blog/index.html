Introduction to Functionalities of the Website

It's adviced to view this website on your computer(to fully make use of the functionalities)

Sidebar

Menu

On the left side of the website, the side bar, you can click on the different directories on the menu such as About, Categories, Tags, Archives, To Do, Search

  • About : Gives basic information about the website and me.

  • Categories : Page where you can check the different types of posts, such as Notes/Thoughts/Challenges etc. Subcategories are provided.

  • Tags : Labels different posts such as "MachineLearning" etc.

  • Archives : Overview of all the posts on the website in time order.

  • To do : Plans for what I want to write / functions that I want to implement in the future. Feel free to give advice!

  • Search : Search specific contents that you want to look for.

Table of Contents

When you view an article, you can click on the sessions under Table of Contents to go to different chapters of the post.

Globe

If you're using a laptop, you might notice the 'Globe' at the bottom of the sidebar. This shows the places where all the visitors of the website came from! Try put your cursor on the globe and zoom in/out using your mouse! Each red dot symbolizes one user!

Posts

  1. Click on read more to read full content of each post!

  2. Word count and estimated Reading time are displayed under the title of each post.

  3. If you find my post interesting, feel free to click on the social media icons at the end of each post to share this post!

  4. You can login your github account to comment each post.

Bottome Bar

The bottom bar provides information about the total wordcount / estimated reading time / the days this website has survived.
